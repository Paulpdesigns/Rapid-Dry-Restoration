The Response Timeline

What happens, hour by hour.

Restoration is a race against absorption and mold growth. Here's the actual sequence your property goes through once you call.

H1

Hour 1

Dispatch arrives, assesses water category and affected area, and begins extraction immediately.

H1–4

Hours 1–4

Standing water is fully removed; air movers and dehumidifiers are placed to begin structural drying.

D1–3

Days 1–3

Daily moisture readings track drying progress; affected materials are treated or removed as needed.

D3–7

Days 3–7

Final clearance testing, then reconstruction begins — drywall, flooring, and paint.
