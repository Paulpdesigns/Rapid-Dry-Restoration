Service 02

Structural Drying

Standing water is only half the problem — moisture trapped in framing, subfloor, and drywall is what causes long-term rot and mold. We dry the structure itself, not just the surface.

Industrial air movers & LGR dehumidifiers
Daily moisture readings, logged
Wall cavity & subfloor drying
Drying verified before closeout

Service 03

Mold Remediation

Mold can establish within 24–48 hours of unaddressed moisture. We contain the area, remove affected material safely, and confirm the space is clear with post-remediation testing.

Physical containment & negative air
HEPA air scrubbing
Safe removal of affected material
Post-remediation clearance test
