Our Story

Built by people who hated the runaround

Our founders spent years subcontracting for larger restoration franchises and kept seeing the same problem: homeowners handed off between an extraction crew, a drying crew, and a mold crew — each one re-diagnosing the job from scratch. Rapid Dry was built to fix that. One licensed team owns your job from the first phone call to the final walkthrough.

Today we're IICRC-certified in water damage restoration, applied structural drying, and mold remediation — and every technician on our trucks carries all three certifications, not just one.
